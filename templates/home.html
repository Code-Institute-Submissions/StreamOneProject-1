Welcome to C Edney Builders

Welcome to our new site for C Edney Builders. Our director is Craig Edney, who with the help of his son Luke and the 2Clix team is joining the 21st Century and getting an online presence to help enhance his already respected reputation as an excellent builder in the Gosport area with over 30 years experience.

Craig started out as a roofer and built his own business which quickly became well respected in the local area. As well as roofing Craig’s companies then started to build excellent extensions, and carried out numerous property renovations (examples of these will soon be listed on the Portfolio section of the site). Craig has handled large contracts for the MOD, Portsmouth City Council and many more which shows the trust and professionalism that has help build his business. C Edney Builders have worked all across the south, including London.

Whilst the site is in its infancy all you really need to know is the Craig’s contact details. C Edney Builders are happy to come and give quotes and advice on possible work opportunities so give us a call on 07770 770600.
